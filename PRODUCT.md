# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Software developers, engineers, and collaborative product teams looking for a fast, simple Kanban and real-time presence workspace without corporate bloat.

## Product Purpose
DevSync provides a lightweight, real-time collaboration space. Success means users can manage tasks and see teammate activity instantly with zero friction or visual cognitive load.

## Brand Personality
*   **Whimsical**: Grounded in the playful "Forest & Acorn" mascot and doodle aesthetics.
*   **Lightweight**: Blazing fast, clean layouts, no unnecessary decoration or heavy borders.
*   **Put-together**: Highly aligned grids, crisp typography, and professional execution.
*   **Warm & Organic Palette**: Rich forest greens, soft oatmeal/linen backgrounds, and accents of light brown, ochre, and golden hues.

## Anti-references
*   **Sterile Enterprise UI**: Dull gray-and-blue grids with zero personality.
*   **SaaS Clichés**: Infinite card-grids with gradient-text headings and tiny all-caps eyebrows.
*   **Amateurish Sketches**: Sloppy, misaligned doodle visuals that undermine confidence.

## Design Principles
1.  **Warmth with Precision**: Visual elements can be whimsical, but layout structures must remain solid, legible, and highly functional.
2.  **Delight in Simplicity**: Focus on micro-interactions and soft color transitions instead of complex widgets or bloated settings.
3.  **Real-time Visibility**: Design features so that live updates, presence indicators, and workspace activity are clear and instantaneous.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
*   Strict adherence to WCAG AA color contrast ratios (≥ 4.5:1 for normal text) on oatmeal/linen backgrounds.
*   Support for `@media (prefers-reduced-motion)` in transitions and cursor tracking.
